Biography

Raluca is a Lecturer in Computer Science at the University of Bradford and a Visiting Researcher at the University of Sheffield, where she is a member of the Testing Group. Her research interests include formal specification methodologies, software testing, especially state-machine based testing using evolutionary approaches, P systems testing and verification. She is a also member of the RoboStar* research group, collaborating in the EPSRC RoboTest project on systematic model-based testing and simulation of mobile autonomous robots.
